The Y Jumps

The Y jumps, located in Provo, Utah are a mainstay in the freeride mountain bike community of Utah and have long provided a beautiful spot to ride and gather. They came to be many years ago when a view college students were looking for a spot to build jumps and trails and push their limits. They since been through many different versions and changes to become what they are today. One thing has stayed true throughout their whole existence and that is that they are founded in a love for the sport and community that surrounds it.
